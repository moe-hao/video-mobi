Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May, 2026

Blue Arcturus ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Blue Arcturus mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing the Services, registering an account, or clicking "I Agree," you acknowledge that you have read this Policy and consent to the collection, storage, and processing of your personal information as described herein.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information collected automatically, and information from third-party sources.

1.1 Information Voluntarily Provided by You

  • Account Information: To create a Blue Arcturus account, we collect device-specific data to generate a unique User ID. You may voluntarily provide additional profile details, including but not limited to a nickname, avatar, gender, and date of birth.
  • Communications: If you contact our customer support, we collect your email address, the contents of your inquiry, and any other information you choose to provide to resolve your request.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and Log Data: When you access our Services, we automatically collect technical information, including hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., IMEI, IDFA, Android ID), IP address, MAC address, and application crash logs.
  • Usage Behavior: We track your interactions with the Services, such as search queries, viewing history, "likes," and playback progress (to facilitate "resume" functionality).
  • Automated Decision-Making (Personalization): We use proprietary algorithms to analyze your viewing preferences and usage patterns to provide personalized content recommendations.

1.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

If you elect to register or log in via third-party platforms (e.g., Facebook, Google, Apple), we receive certain public profile information as authorized by your privacy settings on those platforms.

2. PURPOSES OF PROCESSING

We process your personal information for the following legal and business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and optimize the Services.
  • Personalization: To tailor the content feed to your interests and improve our recommendation engine.
  • Security and Integrity: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity, security breaches, or unauthorized access.
  • Compliance: To enforce our Terms of Service and comply with applicable statutory and regulatory obligations.

3. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may disclose your information in the following circumstances:

  • Authorized Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, data analytics, and content moderation. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Social Sharing: If you choose to share content via third-party applications (e.g., WhatsApp, Facebook), such interactions are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third-party platforms.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of our users or the public.

4. DATA SECURITY AND CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS

  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures, including SSL encryption and restricted access controls, to protect your data against unauthorized access or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
  • International Transfers: Your information is stored and processed on servers located in Hong Kong. By using the Services, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, or country, where data protection laws may differ.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, or as required by applicable law (e.g., for tax or accounting purposes). Upon the expiration of the retention period, we will delete or anonymize your data so that it can no longer be associated with you.

6. YOUR DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Rectification: The right to request copies of your data and correct any inaccuracies.
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): The right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Restriction of Processing: The right to object to or restrict certain processing activities, particularly those based on automated decision-making.

To exercise these rights, please use the in-app feedback tool or contact us at support@bluesrcshow.com.

7. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 14 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If you are a minor, you must obtain the express consent and involvement of a parent or legal guardian to use the Services. We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

Your continued use of the Services after such notification constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
